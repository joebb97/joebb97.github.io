Butler Lampson's System Design Hints

A summary of Butler Lampson ’s original paper 2 Functionality 2.1: Keep it simple Do one thing at a time, do it well. Make small, working, effective interfaces, they are contracts, don’t try to do too much, don’t generalize. Get it right. Write specifications (then implement), document the abstract functionality provided by your application and its interfaces. 2.2: Corollaries Make it fast rather than general or powerful.
